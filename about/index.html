About

Hi, I'm Lisa Ridley, long-time Drupal enthusiast and open source fan girl.  I'm also a NASCAR enthusiast and a University of Tennessee Volunteers fan.  

This blog is a place for my personal thoughts on a wide variety of subjects of interest to me, that may or may not be of interest to others.

This blog is created and maintained with the Tome project, using the Bookish theme.  Many thanks to Sam Mortenson for creating this, it's perfect for blogging when you don't want to maintain a hosted CMS, but you want to use a CMS locally for managing your content.  Thanks Sam!

I may retheme it at some point, then again, I may not.
